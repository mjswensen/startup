Color Palette

For background colors, simply add the appropriate class: bg-red-lighter, bg-red-light bg-red, bg-red-dark, bg-orange-lighter, bg-orange-light, bg-orange, bg-orange-dark, bg-yellow-lighter, bg-yellow-light, bg-yellow, bg-yellow-dark, bg-green-lighter, bg-green-light, bg-green, bg-green-dark, bg-blue-lighter, bg-blue-light, bg-blue, bg-blue-dark, bg-purple-lighter, bg-purple-light, bg-purple, bg-purple-dark, bg-pink-lighter, bg-pink-light, bg-pink, bg-pink-dark, bg-gray-lighter, bg-gray-light, bg-gray, bg-gray-dark, bg-gray-darker, bg-white, or bg-black.

For foreground (text) colors, add the appropriate class: fg-red-lighter, fg-red-light, fg-red, fg-red-dark, fg-orange-lighter, fg-orange-light, fg-orange, fg-orange-dark, fg-yellow-lighter, fg-yellow-light, fg-yellow, fg-yellow-dark, fg-green-lighter, fg-green-light, fg-green, fg-green-dark, fg-blue-lighter, fg-blue-light, fg-blue, fg-blue-dark, fg-purple-lighter, fg-purple-light, fg-purple, fg-purple-dark, fg-pink-lighter, fg-pink-light, fg-pink, fg-pink-dark, fg-gray-lighter, fg-gray-light, fg-gray, fg-gray-dark, fg-gray-darker, fg-white, or fg-black.
